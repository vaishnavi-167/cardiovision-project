Heart-Healthy Lifestyle Tips

Small daily changes can lead to a lifetime of better heart health.

Stay Hydrated

Drinking enough water helps your heart pump blood more easily through the vessels to the muscles.

Active Living

At least 30 minutes of moderate exercise five days a week can significantly improve cardiovascular health.

Better Sleep

Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep. Lack of sleep is linked to higher blood pressure and heart issues.

Manage Stress

Practice deep breathing or meditation to keep your stress levels in check and protect your heart.
