Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about CardioVision and heart health assessment.

How accurate is this risk assessment?

CardioVision uses an AI-based algorithm to calculate risk based on the data you provide. While it follows standard health protocols, it is meant for awareness only and is not a clinical diagnosis. Always consult a doctor for official medical advice.

What is BMI and why does it matter?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. Maintaining a healthy BMI (18.5–24.9) is crucial because being overweight puts extra strain on your heart and increases cardiovascular risks.

How often should I take this assessment?

If your risk is Low, taking the test every 6 months or whenever your lifestyle changes is sufficient. If your risk is Medium or High, you should consult a doctor immediately and monitor your health more frequently.

Is my personal data saved?

No, for this version of CardioVision, we do not store your personal health data on our servers. Your results are calculated in real-time and cleared once you close the session.
