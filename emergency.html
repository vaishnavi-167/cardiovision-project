Warning Signs (Symptoms)

Seek immediate medical attention if you experience:

  • Chest pain, pressure, or discomfort
  • Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
  • Pain radiating to arms, neck, or jaw
  • Sudden dizziness, nausea, or cold sweats

URGENT NOTE: If heart attack symptoms occur, do not wait. Every minute counts. Transport the patient to the nearest hospital immediately. Learning CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation) can save a life in such situations.
